Products that are appropriate for bipolar disorder

Products that are appropriate for bipolar disorder

Bipolar affective disorder (BAR) is a mental illness that is characterized by various emotional States - from manic to depressive episodes. Behavioral therapy, along with medication, helps patients control their emotions. Changing food habits can also have a positive impact on fighting the disease. Below are some products that specialists recommend to patients with BAR.

Whole-grain products.

Wholegrain not only do they have a good effect on the activity of the heart and digestive tract, but they are also suitable for BAR. The reason for this is that these foods are believed to have a calming effect on the brain. There are various foods from this group that can be included in the diet: pasta, brown rice, oatmeal, quinoa and other.

Legume products.

All legumes are rich in magnesium. Some studies show that this mineral contributes to the reduction of manic attacks in patients with BAR. This information has not yet been fully confirmed, but such products are certainly useful, and it is advisable to include them more often in the menu.

Avocado.

In recent years, avocados have become increasingly popular on the market due to their monounsaturated or "good" fatty acids (MFA). These fatty acids help to lower cholesterol levels in the blood and to maintain normal blood pressure. For people suffering BAR, avocados and the MNFCS they contain are useful because they improve the brain's sensitivity to the hormone serotonin and thus reduce the risk of depressive episodes.

Products with high selenium content.

Selenium is a mineral that is very important for brain activity. Scientists believe that it affects the control of emotions. Selenium deficiency is associated with an increased risk of depression and anxiety. A person needs 55 micrograms of selenium per day, which can be obtained from the following products:

- Brazil nut;

- tuna;

- shrimps;

- flounder fillet;

"beef liver.

- dark chocolate.

Dark chocolate with a high cocoa content includes a lot of phenylethylamine, which is believed to help improve the mood of people who suffer from depression or are prone to depressive episodes, such as patients with BAR. However, such a positive effect is provided only by dark chocolate with cocoa content is more than 95%.

Liver.

If you are not a vegetarian, like to eat meat and at the same time suffer from BAR, you will suit the liver. This organ contains vitamin B9 and folic acid, which are involved in the regulation of serotonin.

Foods high in omega-3 fatty acids (O-3 LCD).

O-3 LCDS are very important for brain function. Adding this type of fatty acid to the emotional stabilization medications prescribed for patients with BAR has a positive effect. Since O-3 LCS are good for both heart and brain function, it is useful to include them in the diet. Some of the products with increased content of such fatty acids:

- salmon;

- tuna;

- herring;

- bollocks;

- flaxseed.
